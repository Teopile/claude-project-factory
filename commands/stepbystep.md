Lay out a clear, numbered step-by-step for: $ARGUMENTS

- Each step is one concrete action, in order, with the why if it's non-obvious.
- Call out prerequisites up front and gotchas/decision points inline.
- Make it followable by someone doing it for the first time.
End with how to verify it worked.
