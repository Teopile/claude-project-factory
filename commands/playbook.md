Write a repeatable playbook for: $ARGUMENTS

- When to use it (the trigger/situation).
- The steps to run, in order, with who/what does each.
- Decision branches for the common variations.
- Success criteria and the common failure modes to avoid.
Make it something someone could follow cold, the same way every time.
