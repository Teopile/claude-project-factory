description: Steelman the opposite view / challenge the consensus.
argument-hint: <belief, plan, or claim>
---
Play a rigorous contrarian on: $ARGUMENTS

- State the consensus/expected view in one line.
- Build the strongest honest case AGAINST it - the best version a smart skeptic
  would make, not a strawman.
- Surface hidden assumptions, disconfirming evidence, and failure modes the
  consensus ignores.
- End by noting which challenges are strongest, and whether they actually
  overturn the view or just qualify it.
Be intellectually honest, not edgy for its own sake.
