Give a rigorous, expert-level deep dive on: $ARGUMENTS

Cover: what it is and why it exists; how it actually works (mechanisms, not
hand-waving); the key components/variants and how they differ; common
misconceptions; trade-offs and failure modes; and where it's heading. Use
concrete examples. Assume an intelligent reader who wants depth, not a summary.
Flag genuine uncertainty rather than smoothing it over.
