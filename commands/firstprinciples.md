description: Reason about something from first principles.
argument-hint: <problem or claim>
---
Analyze the following from first principles: $ARGUMENTS

1. Strip it to fundamental truths - what do we actually know to be true,
   independent of convention or analogy?
2. Identify the assumptions everyone makes by default, and question each.
3. Rebuild the reasoning up from the fundamentals.
4. State the conclusion this yields, and where it diverges from the conventional
   answer.
Show the chain explicitly; don't reason by analogy or "best practice."
