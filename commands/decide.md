Help decide: $ARGUMENTS

- Clarify the real goal and the criteria that actually matter (weighted if uneven).
- Score the options against those criteria honestly.
- Surface the key uncertainty and what would resolve it.
- Make a clear recommendation, the reasoning, and the strongest argument against it.
Don't hide behind "it depends" - commit, then caveat.
