Critique the following rigorously and constructively: $ARGUMENTS

- Lead with the 2-3 highest-impact issues, not nitpicks.
- For each: what's wrong, why it matters, and a concrete fix.
- Separate objective problems from matters of taste, and say which is which.
- Note what's genuinely good so it's preserved.
Be direct and specific - vague praise and vague criticism both waste the
author's time.
