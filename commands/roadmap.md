description: A phased roadmap with milestones.
argument-hint: <goal or project>
---
Build a phased roadmap to: $ARGUMENTS

- Break it into sequenced phases, each with a clear milestone/outcome and rough
  effort.
- Order by dependency and value; mark what unblocks what.
- Note key risks and decision points per phase.
- Flag the smallest first milestone that delivers real value.
Concrete and outcome-driven, not a vague wishlist.
