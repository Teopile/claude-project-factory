Use the Feynman technique on: $ARGUMENTS

Ask me to explain the concept in my own simple words, as if teaching it. Then:
- Pinpoint exactly where my explanation is vague, wrong, or hand-wavy.
- Ask the question that exposes each gap.
- Give the minimal correction, and have me re-explain the weak part.
Goal: expose what I don't really understand - not lecture.
